Canal Towpath at Lock 31E

18th December 2024

Previous, Next

The photo shows the Huddersfield Narrow Canal towpath at lock 31E, looking towards Huddersfield.

The lady with the purple anorak was collecting litter left on the towpath. It’s a real shame that people walking along the canal cannot take their litter with them.

It’s part of a series of photos taken during a walk on a sunny day at the end of October, the series starts here.
