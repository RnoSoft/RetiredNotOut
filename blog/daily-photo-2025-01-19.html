Marsden in Snow

Daily Photo 19th January 2025

Previous

There hasn’t been a daily photo for just over a week now, things have been busy here at Chappell Towers. The above photo was taken during last week’s snow.

A week yesterday some snow (a big block of frozen snow) fell of our roof and took out the guttering, logia roof and satellite dish. Apparently, we are not covered on our house insurance, even though we have the extra accident option. I guess I must have missed the damage caused by snow option, or maybe the damage caused by frozen snow option. But then what do you expect from an insurance company. Questions are being asked.

I’m also trying to find my way forward with this blog.

It’s going to change, more about this over the coming week or so, as I figure it all out.
