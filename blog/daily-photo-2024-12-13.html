Country Lane, Marsden

Daily Photo 13th December 2024

Previous

For the next ten days or so, I’m going to publish a series of photos taken during a walk on October 24th. It was a bright sunny autumnal day, and it was still warm. The Leaves where changing colour and falling from trees. There were still narrow boats on the canal.

Recently, the weather here in Marsden has mostly been dark and cloudy. And even on those rare days when we get the sun, it’s very low in the sky.

The photo above is about a quarter of a mile from where I live, on the way to the canal.
