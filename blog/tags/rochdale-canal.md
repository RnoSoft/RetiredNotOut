# Posts About the Rochdale Canal

The [Rochdale Canal](https://canalrivertrust.org.uk/canals-and-rivers/rochdale-canal){:target="_blank"} runs from Sowerby Bridge (Crosley) to Manchester (Castlefield), across the Pennines. It passes through Hebden Bridge, Todmorden and Rochdale on its way.
