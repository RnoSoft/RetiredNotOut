# Posts About the East Lancs Railway

The [East Lancs Railway](https://www.eastlancsrailway.org.uk/){:target="_blank"} is a heritage railway line that runs from Heywood to Rawtenstall, via Bury. It operates steam trains and vintage diesels throughout the year.
