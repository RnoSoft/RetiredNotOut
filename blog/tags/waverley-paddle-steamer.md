# Posts About the Waverley Paddle Steamer

The Waverley is the last sea going paddle steamer in the world. 

Every year [the boat tours the UK](https://waverleyexcursions.co.uk/){:target="_blank"}, taking passengers on cruises. It's well worth going on one.
