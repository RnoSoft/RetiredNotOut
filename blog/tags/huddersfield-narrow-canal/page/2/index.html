Posts About the Huddersfield Narrow Canal

The Huddersfield Narrow Canal runs from the Calder and Hebble Navigation at Cooper Bridge Junction to Manchester, across the Pennines. It passes through Huddersfield, Marsden (where I live) and Uppermill on its way.
