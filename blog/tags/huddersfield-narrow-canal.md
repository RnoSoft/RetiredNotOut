# Posts About the Huddersfield Narrow Canal

The [Huddersfield Narrow Canal](https://canalrivertrust.org.uk/canals-and-rivers/huddersfield-narrow-canal){:target="_blank"} runs from the Calder and Hebble Navigation at Cooper Bridge Junction to Manchester, across the Pennines. It passes through Huddersfield, Marsden (where I live) and Uppermill on its way.
