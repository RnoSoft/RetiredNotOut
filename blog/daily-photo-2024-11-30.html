Liverpool, Across the River Mersey

30th November 2024

Previous, Next

It was taken from the prom in New Brighton, you can just see the Isle of Man Ferry approaching the dock.

There is a lovely walk from the Seacombe Ferry Terminal, along the prom, to New Brighton. It’s a couple of miles and is well worth it. When you reach New Brighton, the Bow Legged Beagle is worth visiting for a few well-earned bevvies. During the week you could do the walk in reverse and catch a late afternoon commuter ferry from Seacombe to the Pier Head. Ferry across the Mersey!

The iron railings run all the way from Seacombe to New Brighton.
