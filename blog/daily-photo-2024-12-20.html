Foot Bridge Over the Canal

20th December 2024

Previous, Next

A bit further on there is a foot bridge over the Huddersfield Narrow Canal. If you cross the bridge and follow the track past the houses, you will eventually reach Sparth reservoir. Once at the reservoir, it’s easy to get back onto the canal.

This photo is part of a series of photos taken during a walk on a sunny day at the end of October, the series starts here.
