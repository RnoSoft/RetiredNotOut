Daily Photo 9th October 2024

Previous, Next

Today was also wet and horrible. My wife and I drove from Porthmadog to Liverpool in very heavy rain.

We then went to the pub, a new one for us, the Liverpool Brewing Company Tap. Its on Lime Street almost opposite the Adelphi Hotel. It’s in Camra’s Good Beer Guide (2025 Edition). The beer was excellent and well-priced. It is now one of our firm favourites. Further details on Camera’s What Pub website.
