Cherry Tree in Flower

3rd April 2025

Previous, Next

I moved to this house, on this day, 22 years ago. There is a cherry tree opposite the house, which I have watched bloom every year. When it blooms, it means the better weather is beginning. That winter is over.

Today promises to be warm and sunny, so I’m off to Dewsbury to see if I can get some photos.
