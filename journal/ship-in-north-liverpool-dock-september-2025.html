Another Ship in a North Liverpool Dock

30th September 2025

Previous, Next

After the Stanley Dock, we drove up the dock road looking for interesting photos. There was only one, shown above. I did this drive last March and only found one interesting photo, I wrote about it here.

The signs banning photos at Everton’s new ground have disappeared, but you still cannot get to the Mersey. We spoke to someone, who said they are still working on the river front, but when all the works are finished it should be possible. Hope so.
