Rochdale Canal, Hebden Bridge

4th May 2025

Previous, Next

This photo is another view along the canal in Hebden Bridge, taken on the 2nd of May during my walk around the town.

I arrived at Hebden Bridge on a bus, got off it near the station, and then followed the canal through town. I left the canal at Hebble End so I could get to the Fox and Goose pub for my first bevvy of the day. The walk along the canal is very pleasant.
