Clough Mill, Marsden in 2013

14th May 2025

Previous, Next

This photo of Clough Mill was taken from almost the same location as yesterday’s, on a dark October morning. It was also taken with my old Canon camera.

These two photos are the only ones I have of the mill, taken from this angle. I wish I had more. But then, I never expected the mill to be demolished. It had been there a long time, and it was part of the scenery. It should have been converted into flats.
