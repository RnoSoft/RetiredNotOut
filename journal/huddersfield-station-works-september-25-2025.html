Huddersfield Station Upgrade, Three

25th September 2025

Previous, Next

Today I went back to Huddersfield Station (again) to get some more photos of its upgrade, almost a week after I took the last set. The new track for platform one has now been laid, the first three photos show the new layout. The station is due to reopen on Monday 29th September.

The next photo shows the site of the new platform. It the top right corner, you can just about make out the mammoth crawler crane, still being dismantled.
