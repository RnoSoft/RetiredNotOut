Photos taken on Family Trips from 2007 to 2012

Recently, I found several sets of photographs taken on various trips I made with my wife, mother and sister.

The first set were taken on a cloudy day in September 2007. One Sunday morning we drove along the Wirral coast from Seacombe to West Kirky, stopping at various places along the way.

In early March 2008 we visited Bury Market and had a train ride along the East Lancashire Railway, were I took these photos.

Later the same year we went to the World War 2 day at Haworth, West Yorkshire, where I took these photos.

In 2010 we all went to Norther France for a holiday, spending two nights in Rouen and one night in Amiens. I took these photos while we were wandering around Rouen. On one of days, we went for a drive along the coast, which resulted in these pictures. The last night of the holiday was spent in Amiens, where I took these photos.

In October 2012, we all went to Brugge for a week, which resulted in these photos. On one of the days, we drove to Oostende, where I took these photos on the pier.
