![River Mersey at the Ferry Tavern, Penketh]

The photo is of the River Mersey at low tide, at the Ferry Tavern, Penketh.

I've not been looking forward to this day, seventy is a lot of years. Mind you, 25,568 days, 613,632 hours, 36,817,920 minutes and 2,209,075,200 seconds old are a lot bigger.

I'm off to Liverpool for drinks at two of my favourite pubs there. The [Vernon Arms](https://camra.org.uk/pubs/vernon-arms-liverpool-165852){:target="_blank"} and [The Ship and Mitre](https://camra.org.uk/pubs/ship-mitre-liverpool-165654){:target="_blank"}.
