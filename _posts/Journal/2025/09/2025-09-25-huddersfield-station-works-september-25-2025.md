![Huddersfield station platforms one and two, one]

ppppppToday I went back to Huddersfield Station (again) to get some more photos of its upgrade, almost a week after I took the [last set](/journal/huddersfield-station-works-september-19-2025). The new track for platform one has now been laid, the first three photos show the new layout. The station is due to reopen on Monday 29th September.

![Huddersfield station platforms one and two, two]

![Huddersfield station platforms one and two, three]

The next photo shows the site of the new platform. It the top right corner, you can just about make out the mammoth crawler crane, still being dismantled.

![The site of the new playforms,]
