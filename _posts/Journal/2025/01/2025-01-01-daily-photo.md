![Sankey Canal, Penketh]

Behind the [Ferry Tavern](/blog/daily-photo-2024-12-31) at Penketh is the [Sankey Canal](https://www.sankeycanal.co.uk/about-sankey-canal){:target="_blank"}{:rel="noopener noreferrer"}, you cross over it to get to the pub. The canal runs from Widnes to Warrington, following the River Mersey, the towpath is a fine walk.

In the background is the Fiddlers Ferry power station. It was decommissioned  in March 2020 as part of net zero and global warming. It is currently being demolished.
